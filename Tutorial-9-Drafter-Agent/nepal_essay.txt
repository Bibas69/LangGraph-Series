Nestled in the Himalayas, Nepal is a land of breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage. From the towering peaks of Mount Everest to the lush Terai plains, its geography is as diverse as its people. Nepal boasts a tapestry of ethnic groups, each preserving unique traditions, languages, and festivals. The nation’s history is marked by the ancient Kirat kingdoms, the Malla dynasties, and the modern republic established in 2008. Its economy thrives on agriculture, tourism, and remittances. Despite challenges like poverty and political instability, Nepal’s resilient spirit and commitment to preserving its natural beauty inspire admiration worldwide.
